How It Works
Four steps.
Under 5 minutes.
No tech skills needed. If you can tap a button, you can export your entire LinkedIn saved library.
⚙️
Step 01
Request Your Data
LinkedIn → Me → Settings & Privacy → Data Privacy → Get a copy → Select "Saved items"
⏱ Takes ~2 min
📧
Step 02
Get the ZIP File
LinkedIn emails you a ZIP file — usually in under 10 minutes. Download it. No need to unzip it.
⏱ Wait ~10 min
📦
Step 03
Upload & Browse
Drop the ZIP into SavedOut. Your full saved library appears instantly — filter by posts, articles, videos, or jobs.
⚡ Instant
📥
Step 04
Export & Read
Select your items, hit export. Download as TXT or Word — with clickable links back to every original post.
✅ Done!
